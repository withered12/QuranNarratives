The Story of Prophet Musa

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

"In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful."

وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَا مُوسَى الْكِتَابَ وَقَفَّيْنَا مِن بَعْدِهِ بِالرُّسُلِ

"And We did certainly give Moses the Scripture and followed up after him with messengers."

[ SURAH AL-BAQARAH : 87 ]
وَآتَيْنَا عِيسَى ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ الْبَيِّنَاتِ وَأَيَّدْنَاهُ بِرُوحِ الْقُدُسِ

"And We gave Jesus, the son of Mary, clear proofs and supported him with the Pure Spirit."
